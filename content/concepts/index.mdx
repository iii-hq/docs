---
title: 'Concepts'
---

The iii engine has three core concepts called Events, Triggers, and Functions.

The Engine also has a few other concepts. They are: Workers, Adapters, and Modules.

All of these concepts are simple but here's what you need to know depending on what you're doing:

- An application developer: [None]

- A highly effective application developer: Events, Triggers, and Functions

- A system architect or implementer: All concepts
